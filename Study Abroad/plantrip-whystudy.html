Top 10 Reasons to Study Abroad


1.
     Learn about yourself and become more self-aware.

2.
     Boost your self-confidence and independence.

3.
     Learn another language.

4.
     Strengthen adaptability, communications, and team-building skills.

5.
     Experienced another culture firsthand and enchance your cross-cultural competency.

6.
     Enhance your career opportunities.

7.
     Make new friends from around the world.

8.
     Expand your worldview.

9.
     Gain a new appreciation for your home country.

10.
   Understand your major in a new way.
